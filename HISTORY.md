# AEGIS Crystal Labyrinth — Complete History

> *From a prototype to a living forest. Six beasts. Fourteen versions. One vision.*

## Timeline

### Phase I: Base (2025)

**Beast 1 — LEVIATHAN** (prototype)
The first breath. A proof of concept showing that projective geometry PG(11,4) could serve as the foundation for a post-quantum cryptographic system. No venoms, no traps, no defense layers. Just the raw mathematical structure — 5,592,405 points in 12-dimensional space over GF(4).

**Beast 2 — KRAKEN** (v10)
The first real system. 5.5 million points, oracle gap of 0.0084, 10 attack types resisted, 287-bit classical security. Runtime: 3.4 seconds. The Kraken proved that statistical indistinguishability between real code columns and decoy columns was achievable. Licensed under BSL 1.1 with the Kraken Clause (permanent ethical restriction). Three auditors: all GO.

### Phase II: Petrification (2025–2026)

**Beast 3 — GORGON** (v16)
The wall of stone. Seven neurotoxic venoms targeting different algebraic attack tools. Gap dropped to 0.0008 — near-perfect statistical invisibility. 18 attack types. The AZAZEL Shuffle randomized venom application order. Gröbner petrification, Knuth semifields, and nested matrioska layers introduced. Runtime: 5.7 seconds (the heaviest beast).

**Beast 4 — AZAZEL** (v5 "The Scapegoat — SONIC BOOM")
The scapegoat that carries the attacker's sins. Seven Hells: Rotten Planks, Rola Bola, Saw Traps, Gorgon Swamp, Fractal Wind, Rain, Judas Echo. Plus: Tilt mechanism (pushes attackers into unfavorable positions), Synthetic Valid Key (a second key that looks real but leads nowhere), Cascade Echo, and Resonance Judas. Runtime collapsed to 2.3 seconds — the fastest beast.

### Phase III: Drain (2026)

**Beast 5 — ACHERON** (v2 "The Desert of Agua Seca")
The river of pain that became a desert. Twelve Desiccation Layers: Osmotic Entropy, Zeno Quicksand, Progressive Dehydration, Oasis of Myrrh, Geothermal Fissure, Code Autophagy, Zeno RAM Paradox, Osmotic Loot, Mirage Heat-Death, Entropy Black Hole, Phase Drift, Rank Echo Collapse. The Epoch Chain made offline simulation mathematically impossible (0/50 match). Judas contradiction rate: 77.1%. Runtime: 3.0 seconds.

**Beast 6 — FENRIR** (v4 "The Viking Wolf")
The chain-breaker. The forest that learns. Eight Mordidas: Gleipnir (fingerprinting), Colmillo de Tÿr (softmax venom blending), Memoria del Lobo (4-phase adaptive psychology), Gleipnir Inverso (phantom neighbor bait), Aullido de Manada (anti-parallelism), Ragnarök (stateless retroactive collapse), Fenrir's Jaw (exponential density throttle), plus M13: El Águila de Sangre (The Blood Eagle — 4-phase ritual execution). Viking Frost amplification. Aikido reflection. Oracle gap: 0.0300. Speed: 0.136 ms/query. Three auditors, four rounds: all GO.
