# AEGIS FENRIR — A Guide for Everyone

## What Is This?

AEGIS FENRIR is a **cryptographic defense system** — software designed to protect secret information from attackers, even attackers using future quantum computers.

Think of it as a living forest that protects a treasure. Authorized visitors (friends) walk in, pick up what they need, and leave safely. Unauthorized visitors (attackers) enter the forest, and the forest watches them, learns their behavior, turns their own tools against them, and ensures they never find the treasure.

## How Does It Work? (The Simple Version)

1. **Friends** have a secret key. They ask for data, they get perfect data. Always. 100%.
2. **Attackers** don't have the key. They receive data that *looks* real but contains invisible poison designed to destroy their mathematical tools.
3. **The wolf watches.** FENRIR identifies *what kind* of attack is being used (there are four major types) and deploys custom-tailored counter-venoms.
4. **The cold accumulates.** Every query the attacker makes increases a "frost" factor that amplifies all defenses. There is no equilibrium. The pain only grows.
5. **The eagle descends.** When the attacker is 92% of the way to cracking the code, a ritual execution sequence activates that makes their progress mathematically irreversible — their computer's memory fills up, their CPU spins endlessly, and their solver crashes.

## Is This Legal?

Yes. FENRIR is a **purely defensive** system. It does not hack back. It does not attack the intruder's infrastructure. It only modifies the data it serves in response to queries. The attacker's tools destroy themselves using information FENRIR provides.

FENRIR is released under a license that prohibits use for surveillance, oppression, weaponization, discrimination, or manipulation of democratic processes.

## What Does "Post-Quantum" Mean?

Current encryption can be broken by quantum computers (which are being built right now). Post-quantum cryptography is designed to resist both current computers and quantum computers. FENRIR provides approximately 287 bits of classical security and over 200 bits of quantum security.
